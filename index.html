About Rock It Bay

Who is Rokit Bay?

Rokit Bay was born in 1984 and is now 38 years old. He was fond of music since childhood. That is why he wrote his music even while studying business management and marketing in the United States for 5 years. After returning to Mongolia, he began to actively produce music. He writes all the tunes and lyrics of his songs himself.

Meaning of his name

The name Rokit Bay is a combination of three different meanings in one word. The main meaning is Rock it Bay. When he was in America, he was called Bay. Another meaning is Rocket Bay. The reason for the rocket is that it is risky, but if it takes off successfully, it means that it will go to unexplored places. But the last value means Raw kit Bay or original sound.
